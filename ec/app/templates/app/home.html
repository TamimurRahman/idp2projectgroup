About BookNest

BookNest is an online bookstore with a mission to financially support local, independent bookshops. We believe that bookshops are essential to a healthy culture, and we're dedicated to helping them thrive.

Every time you buy a book on BookNest, your purchase helps support independent bookshops across the country. We partner directly with shops to ensure they receive the full benefit from every sale.
